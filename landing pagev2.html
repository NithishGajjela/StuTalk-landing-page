About StuTalk

StuTalk is a vibrant club at VBIT dedicated to fostering student engagement, creativity, and leadership. Since its inception in 2007, StuTalk has provided a dynamic platform for students passionate about creative writing, design, and journalism.

Our club actively organizes events that inspire and empower students, while also publishing two annual editions: the Freshmen Edition and the Republic Day Edition, showcasing the diverse talents within our student community.
